Explore the Vibrant City of Barcelona

Barcelona, a city on the northeastern coast of Spain, is known for its art and architecture. The fantastical Sagrada Família church and other modernist landmarks designed by Antoni Gaudí dot the city. Its lively street scene, Mediterranean beaches, and rich history make it a must-visit destination.

Top Attractions to Visit:

  • Sagrada Família
  • Park Güell
  • La Rambla
